Hi there! Welcome to my website!

My name is Somya Agarwal and I am a Certified Expert in Graphic Design and Visualization. I have completed my graduation from Calcutta University and am currently working with Think Surf Media as Graphic Designer. I have developed this website to showcase all my assignments and projects that I have accomplished so far.

I am a creative, independent and detail-oriented professional who works with a variety of clients and caters to their needs with innovative designs and solutions. I have good hands-on experience with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro and Animate . I also have good knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. Feel free to share your feedback and comments.
